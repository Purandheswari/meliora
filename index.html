ABOUT

The Student Inconvenience Reporting System is a dedicated platform designed to empower students and enhance their college experience by providing a convenient and efficient means of reporting problems, concerns, and inconveniences they may encounter while on campus. This system serves as a bridge between students and college authorities, facilitating open communication and quick resolution of issues.

The system is designed with a user-friendly interface to ensure that students of all technical backgrounds can easily navigate and use the platform. Students can report issues through multiple channels, including text, images, videos, or by simply describing the problem. This flexibility allows for more comprehensive reporting.The system categorizes reported issues, making it easier for college authorities to prioritize and address them efficiently. Common categories may include academic concerns, facilities and maintenance, safety, and more. Students receive notifications and updates on the status of their reports, keeping them informed about the progress of issue resolution. The platform collects data on reported issues, allowing colleges to analyze trends and patterns, ultimately leading to proactive improvements in campus facilities and services.
